What is Satisfactory?

Satisfactory is a first-person open-world factory building game by CoffeeStain Studios.

What is FicsItNetworks?

FicsItNetworks is a mod for Satisfactory by Panakotta00 that allows you to control, mointor, manage and automate each process of your factory by providing a network system and programmable computers.
